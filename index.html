Hi, I'm Amy! Welcome to my Official Art Portfolio Website!

I am native to Boulder, Colorado. My life has always been full of color. The four distinct seasons in Colorado exposed me to the vibrant and monotonous scenaries that can dominate your mood. The emotional and energetic impact of colors has always fascinated me.

Despite having spent my early years in Colorado, my art work is profoundly inspired and impacted by Latin influences, of which I was lucky to experience first-handedly in my late teens. In fact, during my college years I studied in Oaxaca, Mexico, and following that I moved to Rio de Janeiro, Brazil where I stayed for 13 years.

The 1960's Tropic√°lia art movement, while widely recognized for the musical evolutions that took place, is one from which my art draws inspiration and as a result, it reflects it. Through this movement's lens I like to use my paintings as a vehicle to reminisce Brazilian cultural focal points, as well as to bring the political undercurrents of the time to the fore.

The bright color schemes and whimsical tones also have striking similarities and draw clear connections to this art movement that revolutionized Brazilian artistic expressions and the social atmosphere of the time.

Colors have always evoked certain emotions and feelings, so I wield them unsparingly to give breath to these same reactions in the viewers.

May this art reach you and move you. This is Art with Heart.
